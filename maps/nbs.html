Nature Based Shorelines Book Adaptation

This is a screenshot of the map I made for the Wisconsin Sea Grant using ArcGIS Experience Builder. You can explore the full "experience" here.

It started as a guide book called "Nature-Based Shoreline Options for the Great Lakes Coasts", written by a team at the Wisconsin Sea Grant, which you can find here. I adapted it to be an online web map app, and added maps to make the case studies more interactive.

Below is a preview of the app, and you may need to sign into arcgis online to view it. If you are a member of UW-Madison, you can sign in with UW-Mad, and then your net id. Not all of the functionality is available on this web page so you can also visit the link I added above!
